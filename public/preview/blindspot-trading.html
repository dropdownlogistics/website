Opening Range Breakout
intraday · 9:30–10:00 window
Wait for the first 30 minutes to set the range. Enter on a clean break of the high or low. Exit at LOD, HOD, or EOD. No chasing.
Swing
multi-day · 2–10 day hold
Daily chart setups only. Enter on a pullback to a moving average or support. Let the trade breathe. Exit signal: break of the trend or target hit.
MA Bounce
daily · 8/21 EMA zone
Price pulls back to the 8 or 21 EMA on the daily. Volume confirms. Enter on the bounce. Clean structure. Clean signal. Let the average do the work.
