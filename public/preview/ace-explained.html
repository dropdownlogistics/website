🍎 The ELI5 Version

You know how a library puts a sticker on every book with a call number so anyone can find it? ACE is that sticker — but for everything you create with AI. Instead of call numbers, each thing gets a name, a type, a version number, and a status (like "active" or "archived"). That's it. That's the whole idea.

How It Works

Three simple pieces

The ACE System
1

The Label

Every important thing gets a simple header: a name, what type it is, what version, and whether it's active or archived. Like a name tag for your work.

2

The Save

When you make something worth keeping, you say "save this." It gets the label and goes into a folder. Simple text files that work everywhere — phone, computer, any AI.

3

The Retrieve

When you start a new conversation, you bring your labels with you. The AI reads them and instantly knows your projects, your preferences, and where you left off. No re-explaining.

✦

That's it.

Label things consistently. Save them in one place. Bring them to new conversations. The AI does the rest.
