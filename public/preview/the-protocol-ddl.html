The Protocol

48 steps that turn a brain with ADHD and Bipolar II into a production system. Every morning. Every evening. No exceptions.

Phase 7: The Route Home
42
Exit to 11th. Right on Grand. Right on 13th. Left on Broadway. Right onto I-35. Stay in middle lane until Johnston Drive exit unless something causes a move. Right lane to prep for 75th.
