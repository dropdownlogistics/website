Party Synergy

The two-person party compensates for low headcount through synergy compression. Feliciano's 10 ft Aura of Protection and Aura of Devotion create a mobile safe zone. Hillie operates from within that radius, firing four-beam Eldritch Blasts while protected by his saving throw buff and charm immunity.

The combat loop: Hillie Hexes a target → Feliciano engages and holds position → Hillie blasts from 10 ft behind him → Feliciano Smites on crits or high-value targets → Lay on Hands when damage overwhelms → Repeat.
