DexInsight

The part titles aren't accidental. The book is structured using audit terminology — Material Weakness, Going Concern, Unqualified Opinion, Reasonable Assurance — because recovery and audit follow the same logic: you assess the damage, test the controls, document the evidence, and issue an opinion on whether the system can continue operating. The memoir isn't a metaphor for systems thinking. It is systems thinking, applied to a life.
