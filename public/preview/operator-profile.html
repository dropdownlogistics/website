I Identity & Operating Context

CPA. 10+ years internal audit. Systems thinker by instinct. Diagnosed Bipolar II + ADHD (post-catastrophic episode March 2024). Medicated, monitored, stable. Eight years sober. Married.

This document exists because Dave operates at a cognitive speed and structural density that AI systems frequently misread as instability. The issue is not velocity. The issue is calibration baseline.
