Your campaign is generating data.
You're just not reading it.

Every session produces rolls, decisions, consequences, and momentum. Right now that data lives in your head, in scattered notes, in a Google Doc you haven't updated since session 3. And you're losing signal.

NPCs forget their own motivations. Quest threads stall without anyone noticing. One player dominates the spotlight while the other goes quiet. The dice feel cursed but you don't know if they actually are. These are governance failures, and every DM has them.

BlindSpot doesn't run your game. It doesn't generate your story. It reads your campaign's data and tells you what you're missing — the blind spots that kill campaigns from the inside.

I built this using the same dimensional analytics engine I use for enterprise audit coverage analysis. Same fact tables. Same dimension tables. Same heatmap matrix. The data changes. The engine doesn't care.

You run the campaign. BlindSpot runs the numbers. The brief tells you what to do about it.
