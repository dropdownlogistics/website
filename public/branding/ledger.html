01 The Card Three data sources. One credential.
01 — ENGAGEMENT RECORD
Completed engagements, scoped and staffed
Every engagement on the card is COMPLETED status in AuditForge. PLANNING engagements show as in-progress. The badge doesn't fire until the work is done.
02 — CONTROL RECORD
Controls tested, key controls, frameworks covered
Only APPROVED and REVIEWED controls count toward the verified stat. DRAFT controls are excluded. The standard governs what counts.
03 — COMPETENCY TOKENS
Governed strengths — ratified, not self-reported
STD-AUDITOR-001 defines 8 strength and 8 weakness tokens per auditor. Assigned at onboarding, reviewed annually. Not a self-assessment.
