The Mark — Exception
No stamp. The graph is the mark.
Excelligence is a documented exception to the DDL stamp system. AuditForge has its AF seal. Knowledge Vault has its KV seal. Excelligence has a graph — 105+ governed entries connected by 200+ typed edges. The force-directed graph explorer is the identity. A stamp would compete with it. The EX monogram is available as a reduction mark only (favicon, 32px and below).
BRAND EXCEPTION · Ratified
The Excelligence graph IS the visual centerpiece. Any brand touchpoint larger than 32px should feature the graph (force-directed layout, gold nodes on navy) rather than a traditional stamp. The graph reads “this is a living, governed structure” at a glance — no mark can replicate that.
