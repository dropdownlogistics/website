01 — Primary Mark
The Council Constellation

DexVerse demanded more than a standard stamp. The Council Constellation extends the DDL stamp system with three additional structural layers — council ring, corpus ring, and circuit traces — representing the 10 council seats, the corpus they review, and the retrieval paths that connect them. The center dot is the model. The ring is the council. The outer stamp is the system.

08 — Usage Rules
Do / Never
✓ Always
  • Violet #8A6CC9 as the DexVerse accent — always
  • Violet-light #A98FE0 specifically for Dex Jr. and the local node distinction
  • The council ring (r=46) with all 10 seat dots when the full mark is used
  • Circuit traces at low opacity — they are architectural, not decorative
  • "DexVerse" as one word — not "Dex Verse" not "dexverse" in prose
  • Seat 1010 in violet-light in all council visualizations — it is the only local node
  • Navy #0D1B2A backgrounds — dark mode is not a mode, it is the default
✗ Never
  • Rush the DexVerse mark — it is cathedral work, it got cathedral treatment
  • Use crimson #B23531 as a DexVerse accent — that belongs to the DDL parent brand
  • Reduce the council dots below 32px without the reduction mark spec
  • White backgrounds — not on DexVerse surfaces, not anywhere
  • Place Dex Jr. as a primary OrbitalHero node — it is a sub-node of DexVerse
  • Use "DV" monogram in body text — it is a mark element only
