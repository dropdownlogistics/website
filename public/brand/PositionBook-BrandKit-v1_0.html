OG Dimensions & Tone
Analytical. Sharp. Evidence-based.
PositionBook is not a hype product. It is not a signals-selling product. It is a logging layer and a pattern surface. The tone is calm and precise — the tone of someone reading a journal, not a tipster.

Tone — Do
Evidence. Pattern. Tracking. R-multiple. Expectancy. Drawdown. Never predict; describe what the log shows. Let the data carry the weight.
Tone — Don’t
No “guru.” No “to the moon.” No signal subscriptions. No dollar-bag emojis. No promises of returns. Not even a winking one.
D&A Family Rule
Green in the product UI. Amber if DDL is referencing PositionBook from outside. Do not flatten the two greens of BlindSpot — different product, different context.
