Brand Voice
Tone & Copy Principles

What we sound like

  • Direct. No hedging. The data says what it says.
  • Analytical without being cold — we're on your side
  • Short sentences. Strong verbs. No filler.
  • Confident but not arrogant — the work proves it
  • We use "you" — this is personal finance, not a lecture

What we don't sound like

  • Hype-y or promotional — we're not selling dreams
  • Overly technical — the insight matters, not the math
  • Preachy about gambling — we're not your sponsor
  • Vague or hedged — "might" and "could" are weak
  • Generic SaaS — no "empower" or "unlock your potential"

Brand Rules
Do / Don't

Do

  • Use Dave Green (#22C55E) as the dominant accent
  • Pair Space Grotesk headlines with JetBrains Mono data
  • Use Source Serif 4 italic for key emotional phrases
  • Dark backgrounds only — #0D2137 or deeper
  • Keep copy direct, short, data-grounded
  • Reference the D&A tagline: signal → structure → edge
  • Credit Dropdown Logistics in the footer

Don't

  • Use white or light mode backgrounds
  • Use Inter, Roboto, or system fonts
  • Use gold/amber as the primary accent (that's DDL)
  • Use purple gradients or generic AI aesthetics
  • Write hype copy — no "unlock", "empower", "supercharge"
  • Promote gambling — we track, we don't encourage
  • Separate the helix mark from the D&A identity
