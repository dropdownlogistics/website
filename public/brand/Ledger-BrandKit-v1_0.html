Tone — Do
Verified. Governed. Portable. Source-linked. “The badge fires when the work is done.” Let the data speak; keep adjectives spare.
Tone — Don’t
No “rockstar,” no “guru,” no resume adjectives. Never imply unverified stats. Never bury the source — the card shows where every number came from.
Badge Rule
Three badge types only: AuditForge Verified (amber), Third-Party Connected (steel), Self-Reported (cream). Every card labels its source. No ambiguity.
